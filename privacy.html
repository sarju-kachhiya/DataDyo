Privacy Policy

At Datadyo, we’re committed to supporting your business growth—whether through better monetization or increased traffic. Our expert team is here to assist you at every stage. Just fill out our contact form, and we’ll respond within 24 hours.

At Datadyo, accessible via www.datadyo.com, we place a high priority on safeguarding the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your personal information.

If you have any questions or require further clarification, feel free to contact us directly.

Consent

By using our website, you agree to this Privacy Policy and consent to our collection and use of your information in accordance with its terms.

Information Collection

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide, and we always make the purpose of collection clear at the point of entry.

When you contact us directly, we may collect details like your name, email address, phone number, and any content you include in your message.

If you register for an account, we may request additional details such as your company name, address, and other contact information.

How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and maintain our website
  • Improve and customize your user experience
  • Analyze user behavior and trends
  • Develop new features and offerings
  • Communicate updates, marketing messages, and customer support
  • Send relevant emails
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity

Log Data

We utilize server log files, which may include your IP address, browser type, ISP, timestamp, and navigation patterns. This data is used strictly for analytical and administrative purposes and is not linked to personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Advertisers

Some ads on our site may be served by partners who use cookies, JavaScript, and web beacons. These tools may automatically capture your IP address to help tailor ad content and measure campaign performance. Datadyo does not control these third-party tracking technologies.

External Privacy Policies

Our policy does not cover third-party websites or advertisers. For detailed practices, please refer to their respective privacy policies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Children’s Privacy

We take child safety seriously. Datadyo does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe your child has shared personal information with us, please contact us promptly so we can remove it.
